SOCIAL MEDIA & MARKETING, ASSOCIATE MANAGER

***DISCLAIMER***
Only read on if you are an over-achiever, data driven, OCD, and are awkwardly creative.

WHO ARE WE?
The Legacy of You (T-LoY) produces inspiring media & entertainment targeted towards emerging thought-leaders and artists. T-LoY's clients include StarQuest Performing Arts Competition - a leader in the dance competition circuit, heading into its 25th season of a 60+ city national tour, hosting 40,000 dancers per season.

WHAT IS THE JOB?
We are looking for an associate to run the Digital Media Experience for our 4-tour operation.

This is an amazing position for anyone looking to become more involved in loyalty-based marketing, digital media, and producing. At this time, the primary focus for this position is around the StarQuest brand however, there may be opportunities to manage other T-LoY adventures. This is a year-round, full time, long-term position that is intended to evolve into a full management position.

PRIMARY TASKS:

**Project Management**
- Plan, implement and execute media calendars
- Analyze KPIs and metrics around all social media initiatives
- Assist in selecting and creating editorial and website content
- Liaise with video editors, bloggers, and designers
- Coordinate with media outlets, conventions, and road teams
- Oversee Facebook advertising & Display Network campaigns

**Admin & Marketing**
- Proof ALL documents and collateral before publishing
- Write marketing copy for public announcements and newsletters
- Respond to incoming social media messages and reviews
- Generate weekly analytics reports: Social, Web traffic, Blog, etc...
- Perform traditional marketing duties as needed: Print, direct mail, conventions, etc...
- Oversee the hiring, training, and management of 2 interns

SUPER HERO POWERS NECESSARY

- Creative and thoughtful copy writing and pRoofreeEding skills (Very Important)
- Strong project management skills - Proven track record to stay on deadline and under budget
- Expansive working knowledge of social media platforms and how to advertise on them, including Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
- Comfortable with Mac iOS systems, including creating basic graphics, minor editing through iMovie, and using email-marketing programs
- Unquenchable thirst to provide customer service and an appreciation for good hospitality
- Knowledge of the dance world is a huge plus, but not a deal breaker


BASIC KNOWLEDGE OF THE FOLLOWING IS PREFERRED
- Microsoft Excel
- Facebook Power Editor
- Google Analytics
- Google Display Networks
- Basics of Wordpress
- Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube,

DATES & DETAILS

The Legacy of You/StarQuest NYC Office is in Midtown Manhattan - centrally located across from Bryant Park @ 42nd Street. We are a boutique, results-oriented office. Traditional hours are Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM with some flexability. On-call, extended or non-traditional working hours and/or travel may be necessary.

Starting compensation is $500-$600 per week with room to grow as we achieve success. Opportunities for additional commissions & bonuses exist after 6 months

For more information:
www.StarQuestDance.com
www.TheLegacyOfYou.com
