Hello! We are looking for an enthusiastic full-time in-house Digital Production Designer who lives for design, loves to collaborate and can create awesome, engaging and lively digital content for the web and social media on the fly.

You will work directly with our Digital Art Director and our Copywriter to execute impactful designs for multiple digital platforms, (e.g. websites, landing pages, email campaigns, social media and various other digital creative.)

REQUIREMENTS:

• Advanced skills in Photoshop and Illustrator
• Experience with wireframing and general knowledge of UX/UI concepts
• Experience using WordPress CMS
• General knowledge of the digital space and visual design strategies
• Experience designing web pages which can translate into responsive HTML
• Experience creating graphics for social media and blog posts
• A good eye for choosing imagery and stock photos for various purposes
• Knowledge, and love, of creating GIFs
• Experience with motion graphics (i.e. Adobe Premiere + After Effects)
• Start-up or agency experience
• A passion for fun, fitness, music, dance or dance cardio
• A glass half-full, positive, can-do attitude (no drama, egos or haters)

We are a tight-knit team of artists and creators, and we are looking for a valuable, down-to-earth addition to our team. You must be able to commute to our office in Venice for all work.

If this sounds like you, please read more about the position and apply at:
